This folder consists of the comparing method and the graphs in the manuscript.

'f_k_c.m', 'medfilt_c.m', and 'tp_c.m' are for the Fourier transforming, median filtering, and Radon transforming respectively.

'loog.m' is for the AGC by taking the logarithm.

'randnoise.m' processes adding the noise to the model data.

'readsgy.m' and 'writesgy.m' are for the inputting and outputting the '.sgy' files.

In 'graphs.m', the colormap is determined by the matrix from 'color.csv'.
